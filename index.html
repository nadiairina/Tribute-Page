The New Sensation

Golden Globes 2025: CHALLENGERS Star Zendaya in Louis Vuitton.


Zendaya Maree Stoermer Coleman is an American actress and singer.

Her accolades include two Primetime Emmy Awards and a Golden Globe Award.

In 2022, Time named her one of the 100 most influential people in the world.

Here's a few of Zendaya's most successful projects:

  • 2010-2013 - Shake It Up
  • 2012 - Frenemies
  • 2014 - Zapped
  • 2015-2018 - K.C. Undercover
  • 2017 - Spider-Man: Homecoming
  • 2017 - The Greatest Showman
  • 2019 - Spider-Man: Far from Home
  • 2019-present - Euphoria
  • 2021 - Malcolm & Marie
  • 2021 - Dune
  • 2021 - Spider-Man: No Way Home
  • 2024 - Dune: Part Two
  • 2024 - Challengers
